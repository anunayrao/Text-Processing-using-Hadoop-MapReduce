Hamburg have beaten Liverpool and Swansea City to land Dutch winger Ola John on loan.
talkSPORT told you earlier this month that Brendan Rodgers and his former club were both in pursuit of John, who has been a peripheral figure at Benfica this term.
However, both Premier League clubs have been gazumped by Hamburg, according to German newspaper Hamburger Morgenpost.
The Bundesliga side have moved for the 21-year-old after Maxi Beister suffered a knee injury last week.
John, who has been capped twice, had been on the verge of returning to Holland to join Ajax, but the Amsterdam giants were unwilling to pay his wages in full.
