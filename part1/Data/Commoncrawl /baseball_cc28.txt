Braves Baseball Summer Camps offer young ballplayers the unique opportunity to learn new skills through the official Braves Camp curriculum and get closer to the team they love! Join us for nonstop baseball fun with an emphasis on teamwork, attitude, and healthy competition. Our unique baseball curriculum will help players of all abilities develop both on and off the field while teaching lessons such as resiliency, optimism and goal setting. 
Our program is divided into five levels. Players are grouped on teams that teach age appropriate skills and offer the right level of competition. Please read more about our curriculum here.
