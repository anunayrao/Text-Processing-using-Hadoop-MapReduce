The Atlanta Braves and Delta Air Lines continue their partnership to honor the legacy of Jackie Robinson by hosting the Atlanta Braves Jackie Robinson Invitational. The event educates youth baseball and softball players about the impact of Jackie Robinson's achievements and allows teams the opportunity to showcase their talent and compete against other teams.
On Saturday & Sunday, April 14th and 15th, 2018, sixteen teams ages 6-12 years old will gather at the Atlanta Braves Baseball Academy located at the Villages at Carver Family YMCA to celebrate Robinson's legacy during this annual tournament. The format of the tournament will be two pool play games that lead into a single elimination bracket to determine a winner.
