LONDON, England - Thursday night saw the WTA Pre-Wimbledon Party presented by Dubai Duty Free roll into town, accompanied by the usual glitz, glamour and gizmos.
And with a stellar line-up from the worlds of sport, music and entertainment there was plenty for the assembled media to get excited about. Read on to find out what The Daily Telegraph, Tatler, Hello! and, erm, Gizmodo, had to say about the annual shindig.
British fashion and lifestyle bible Tatler sampled the atmosphere and canapés alongside Serena, Sir Richard and company.
With Wimbledon just a few days away, last year's finalist Eugenie Bouchard was suitably dressed all in white. The Daily Telegraph, Hello!, BT Sport and The Evening Standard ran the rule over the evening's fashion choices.
Meanwhile The Daily Mail turned it's attention to the party's glamor couple, Ana Ivanovic and Bastian Schweinsteiger.
Last, but by no means least, tech website Gizmodo took a closer look at party sponsor Jaguar's innovative bioreactive 'purple carpet'.
