It’s the transfer saga that is set to drag on all summer, despite Jose Mourinho’s intentions, and there are yet more twists in Paul Pogba’s transfer to Manchester United.
There were reports that the only thing holding up the Frenchman’s move was the fee due to his agent Mino Raiola.
Raiola stands to make up to £25m from the deal, which he believes Juventus are contracted to pay, although they are refusing to pay him a penny.
United have offered a compromise where they will pay half of it, but Juve do not want to give him anything for taking away their brightest star.
