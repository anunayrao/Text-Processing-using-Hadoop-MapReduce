In Oyster Bay Cove, N.Y., a 4,230-square-foot, five-bedroom, four-full-and-two-half-bath house built in 1927, with four fireplaces, dual staircases, French doors, arched doorways, a solarium, a pool and a tennis court, on 6.57 acres.
In Pennington, N.J., a five-bedroom, four-and-a-half-bath, 6,358-square-foot expanded 1820 farmhouse, with a formal center hall, parlor and dining room, a bright sunroom, a third floor playroom and a detached two-car garage with a two-bedroom apartment upstairs, on 2.3 acres with a pond and creek.
